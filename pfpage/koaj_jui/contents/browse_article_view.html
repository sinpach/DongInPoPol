ABSTRACTABSTRACTABSTRACTABSTRACTABSTRACTABSTRACTABSTRACTABSTRACTABSTRACTABSTRACT

A total of 168 non-English major students and 30 college English teachers from a top university in Mainland China participated in the study. Two questionnaires were distributed to our student and teacher participants, respectively, and a semi-structured interview was organized afterwards. It was found that neither the students nor the teachers have sufficient knowledge about ELF, despite their awareness of the lingua franca English.
